We process your data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG), and applicable data protection laws.

3. Detailed Description of Data Processing

3.1 Account Registration and Authentication

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored in encrypted/hashed form). If you use Apple Sign-In, we receive a unique identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email from Apple.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract).

3.2 App Content Data

Data you enter into the App (properties, units, tenants, financing details, notes) is stored to provide the App's core functionality. This data is processed on your device and, when using an account, synchronized with our backend.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract).

3.3 Technical and Usage Data

We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support and to ensure compatibility. We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs that track your behavior across apps or websites.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in providing a stable service).

4. Third-Party Processors

4.1 Supabase

We use Supabase (Supabase Inc.) for authentication and cloud database storage. Supabase processes data on our behalf as a processor under a data processing agreement (Art. 28 GDPR). Data is stored in the European Union (EU). Supabase's privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.2 Apple Services

When you use Apple Sign-In, Apple processes your data in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. We only receive the data you choose to share (e.g., name, email). Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4.3 Apple App Store

Downloads and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, unless we are required to retain certain data for legal obligations (e.g., tax, commercial law) or for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR): You may request information about the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): You may request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR): You may request that we restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR): You may request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR): You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR): Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

Account deletion: You can delete your account at any time within the App (Settings → Delete Account). This will permanently remove your account and associated data from our systems.

Complaint to supervisory authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. For Germany: The competent authority for our company is the Landesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz Niedersachsen, Prinzenstraße 5, 30159 Hannover. For other EU countries: the supervisory authority of your place of residence.

Contact: To exercise your rights, contact us at info@papas-kreditkarte.com. We will respond within one month.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Secure authentication (password hashing, OAuth)
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security assessments

8. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). Where we use service providers outside the EEA (e.g., Supabase may use sub-processors), we ensure appropriate safeguards such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46(2)(c) GDPR) or adequacy decisions.

9. Children

The App is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete such data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.
